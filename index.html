How Ancestry Gotra Verification Works

To preserve our lineage guidelines and traditional values, every applicant must pass a strict verification process managed by Mewar community elders.

1

Gotra Details Submission

Applicants provide complete gotra charts covering Self, Mother, Paternal Grandmother (Dadi), and Maternal Grandmother (Nani).

2

Elders Manual Review

Mewada Rajput community elders manually cross-verify details with ancestral charts (Vanshavali) to confirm authentic lineages.

3

Verified Profile Badge

Upon approval, the profile receives a Verified Shield badge and is displayed to other registered members.
